Lasagna

Description

Love lasagna, but do enjoy all the prep work and clean up the comes with it? Try placing the items in a crockpot, yes you heard me, just stack all the ingredients into a slow cooker to reduce the work needed. It will take twice as long compare to baking but less mess.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • 1lb of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1lb of Mozzeralla
  • 8oz of Cottage Cheese or Ricotta
  • Any vegetable you may want to add

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. While ground beef is cooking, shred you cheese
  3. Drain and season you ground beef; set aside
  4. If sauteing veggies, do so now
  5. In a large bowl; combine beef, veggies, and sauce
  6. Place a spread of the combined sauce on the bottom of the slow cooker
  7. Layer noodles on the bottom
  8. Spread either the ricotta or cottage cheese on the noodles
  9. Dump a generous helping ontop of the noodles
  10. Spinkle cheese
  11. Repeat until no more meat sauce is left
  12. Finish with a helping of cheese
  13. Cook low for 8-10 hours or high for 6-8
  14. Enjoy
