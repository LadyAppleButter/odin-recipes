Chicken and Rice

Description

Quick and easy meal for the week. Nice thing about this recipe is that any type of rice can be use and any part of the chicken can be used.

We use chicken thighs and broccoli and cheese rice packets more than anything else.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 chicekn thighs
  • 1 Seasoned rice packets
  • 2 cups of water
  • a spoonful of 'Better than Bouillon"

Steps

  1. Spray insert of slow cooker with cooking spray
  2. Dump the rice packet into the slow cooker
  3. Pour 2 cups of water into the slow cooker
  4. Add a spoonful of "Better than Bouillon"; stir
  5. Add chicekn thighs
  6. Cook covered on high for 3-4 hours
  7. Enjoy
